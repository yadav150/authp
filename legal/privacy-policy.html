Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This project is a demonstration. No personal data is actually stored or shared. In a production environment, your email, name, and authentication tokens would be processed securely via Firebase and never sold or misused.

The auth forms collect only the data you enter. No cookies or tracking are used beyond Firebase’s required session tokens.
